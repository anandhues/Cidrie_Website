The Chundan Vellam,the integral part of Vallom Kalli,which is the traditional boat race of Kerala,it is also known as the Kerala's Snake Boat and is the one of the major tourist attractions and iconic symbol of the state. Vallom Kalli is a form of canoes racing which uses paddled war canoes and is mainly conducted during the season of the harvest festival onam in spring. Vallom Kalli is spot that keralite hold close to there heart and the chundan vellom is the representation of it. This miniture model of chundan vellom made by the local artians can be gfted as a souenir or use in home or office decor.
